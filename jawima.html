This project is my main outlet when it comes to music and sound. I write, produce, mix and master audio under the name Jawima, and I've been doing that since 2015 (more seriously from 2019 and on). Since 2021, I have been collaborating with the people over at Acrylic Label who has helped me with distribution and marketing. This has resulted in continious support from big playlists such as Lofi Beats by Spotify and BEATstrumentals by Apple Music.

The more recent music that I've released have been scheduled projects with deadlines and close collaboration with the team over at Acrylic Label.

Collabs with:

Acrylic Label, Desci, Orestes Gomez, Strong Maurice, Jussin, and more...

Main takeaway

This project has taught me a lot about the importance of small details in a production, aswell as how to learn a new skill. It has also helped me learn to take initiative when it comes to networking and finding opportunities.
