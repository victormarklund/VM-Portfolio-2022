Bloodmoon

Tools: Affinity Designer, Illustrator
Role in team: UI and wireframes
2021

This project was part of a course I took at Ume√• university. The goal was to conceptualize a campaign towards destigmatizing menstruation and the design was to include the Pantone color "Period". The concept that my team came up with ended up being Bloodmoon, which partly consisted of a social gamification based on the old myth that menstrual cycles can sync with the people one surrounds oneself with, as well as the lunar phases. Our concept also included a website which would host educational content surrounding menstruation, as well as a forum where users could talk about their experiences.

Look and feel

My role in this project was to lead the production of the website UI and high/medium fidelity wireframes.

The home page mainly consisted of a large call to action aswell as some matching illustrations.

The learn page provided users with a simple design to quickly navigate to different types of education.

Example of the video page.

The about page.

The forum where users could chat and create threads. This page used an inverted colorscheme in order to clearly distinguish the forum from the informative website.

Main takeaway

This was a fun project were I had to put my creative muscles to work. It also took place during the midst of the covid-19 pandemic, which led the project to become a remote collaboration were we often worked synchronously. This taught me a lot about the common challanges with remote work, but also how to get through these and still end up being productive.
