Lemon Juice Collective

Tools: Discord, Trello, Instagram, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects
Role in team: founder, administration, planning and visuals
2020-2021

This project aimed to build a community and a creative space, where people from all over the world could meet, learn and grow together. Lemon Juice Collective served as one of my hobby projects during my studies, and with the help of two of my good friends, I formed a little team. The goal of this team was to plan and host events for the community to participate in, as well as to futher improve the digital space where we hung out.

The main platform for this project ended up being a Discord server where community members could chat, post projects for feedback, spam memes, etc.

The main event that was hosted on the server was Word of the Week. This challange provided the members with a prompt which they were to use as inspiration for a piece of artwork. The artwork could be anything they wanted and the level of ambition didn't matter. The goal was to inspire people to just make something. Main takeaway

This project eventually ended in late 2021 when I shifted focus onto other endeavours. It taught me a lot about the weight of progressive testing in order to get users onboard with your ideas. It also gave me the hard lesson that even though your idea might seem great, it might not work at all in the real world.
